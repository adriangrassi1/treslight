THE BEGINNING
Before the van,
there was the road.

My name is Adrian. I'm half Italian, half Norwegian — which means I grew up between two very different ideas of what life should look like. I chose the one that moves.

In 2023, I bought a one-way ticket to Australia. No plan, no return date. Just a backpack and eleven months of roads, people, and places I hadn't imagined yet.

"Eleven months. One backpack. Australia showed me that the slower you go, the more you actually see."

From Australia I flew to New Zealand — South Island. I converted a small van and drove for weeks through some of the most remote landscapes I've ever seen. That's where van life stopped being an idea and became a way of being.

THE T5
A night train
and a decision.

Back in Europe, I knew I wanted to do it again — but this time with something more. A real home on wheels.

Late September 2025, a few days before my birthday, I found it by accident on a German website. A 2012 Volkswagen California T5, full camper setup, 4x4, 105,000 km. The kind of listing that disappears overnight.

I didn't think twice. That night I took a train to Bremen, in northern Germany, and by the next morning the California was mine.

"A night train to Bremen. A birthday present to myself I hadn't planned."

A few weeks later I pointed it south — France, Spain, Portugal. Treslight had begun.
